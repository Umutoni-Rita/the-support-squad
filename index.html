Our mission, the problem,
and what we're building

🎯

Mission

To improve Rwanda's education through arts, design, and culture — creating youth entrepreneurs who transform Rwandan heritage into global opportunity.

📌

The Problem

Over 90% of Rwanda's workforce lacks adequate soft skills (African Development Bank, 2024). Despite the CBC curriculum's promise, teachers default to rote instruction — hundreds of thousands of students graduate without ever using Rwanda's rich creative heritage as a tool for growth.

If not addressed by 2030, Rwanda risks losing a generation that could have turned its cultural richness into opportunity.

💡

Our Solution

We tap into the untouched creative and cultural foundation already within Rwandan traditions, crafts, and artistic identity — turning them into a curriculum for entrepreneurship and self-expression.
