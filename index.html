Who we are

Our mission, the problem,
and what we're building

Mission

To improve Rwanda's education through arts, design, and culture therefore creating youth entrepreneurs who transform Rwandan heritage into global opportunity.

The Problem

Over 90% of Rwanda's workforce lacks adequate soft skills (African Development Bank, 2024). Despite the CBC curriculum's promise, teachers default to rote instruction — hundreds of thousands of students graduate without ever using Rwanda's rich creative heritage as a tool for growth.

If not addressed by 2030, Rwanda risks losing a generation that could have turned its cultural richness into opportunity.

Our Solution

Inzozi delivers in-school art, design, and cultural skills training in secondary schools to identify talented students. Top performers advance to hands-on professional practice, where they create marketable products inspired by Rwandan tradition under expert mentorship. These products are sold through the Inzozi marketplace, with creators earning 68% of sales while 32% is reinvested to fund future training programs.
